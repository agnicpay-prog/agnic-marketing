# Campaign: "Is Your Practice Invisible to AI?" Workshop

Owner skill: [events](../skills/events/SKILL.md)

Priority tier: 1 (with tier 2 secondary benefit if builders attend/co-host)

## Objective
Run one live workshop for local business owners; convert 30%+ of attendees to a booked Audit.

## Audience
Local clinic/practice owners in one metro area (start local, in-person if possible — trust matters more than reach here).

## Bottleneck being addressed
Cold outbound alone is slow; a live, in-person demonstration compresses trust-building into one session.

## Core message/hook
"Come watch us ask ChatGPT to find a business like yours — live — and see if you show up."

## Channels (ranked)
1. Local business association / chamber of commerce partnership to source attendees.
2. Direct invites from founder's warm network.
3. LinkedIn event promotion.

## Assets needed
- Workshop agenda (see [../templates/workshop.md](../templates/workshop.md))
- Live-audit demo script (pull 2-3 volunteer businesses from the room)
- Follow-up email for attendees who don't book on the spot (see [../templates/email.md](../templates/email.md))

## Timeline
2-3 weeks lead time to source 15-20 attendee commitments; 1 session; follow-up within 48 hours.

## Owner
Events Lead for logistics; founder presents (this is a founder-brand and trust-building moment, don't delegate the live demo).

## KPIs
- Attendees (target: 15-20, quality over quantity)
- Audits booked same-day or within 48 hours (target: 30%+ of attendees)

## Budget
Venue/refreshments cost only if not hosted via a partner association; otherwise near-zero cash cost.

## Kill criteria
If attendee sourcing can't reach 10 confirmed local business owners within 2 weeks, don't run it as a standalone event — fold the demo into 1:1 outreach instead.

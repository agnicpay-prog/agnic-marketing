# Campaign: AI Builder Partner Program Recruiting

Owner skill: [builder-relations](../skills/builder-relations/SKILL.md)

Priority tier: 2

## Objective
Recruit 5 active partners (defined as: closed or actively selling at least one deal) within 45 days.

## Audience
AI builders, AI consultants, automation agencies, n8n builders, LangGraph developers, MCP developers, AI freelancers — prioritize people already in the founder's network first.

## Bottleneck being addressed
Partners sign up but don't sell — there's no "first deal in a week" path yet.

## Core message/hook
"You already sell AI work. Add one more thing your clients need — getting found by AI — and take a cut, with zero delivery work."

## Channels (ranked)
1. Founder's direct network (DMs, warm intros) — fastest path to first 3-5 partners.
2. Builder communities (n8n, LangGraph/MCP forums and Discords) — value-first participation, see the [community](../skills/community/SKILL.md) skill.
3. Content: a LinkedIn/X post explaining the program, aimed at builders.

## Assets needed
- Partner one-pager (offer + commission structure, one page)
- First-deal starter kit (outreach template, objection handling, sample audit) — adapt from [../templates/email.md](../templates/email.md)
- Recruiting post for LinkedIn/X (see [../templates/linkedin.md](../templates/linkedin.md))

## Timeline
Days 1-5: build one-pager + starter kit. Days 6-20: direct outreach to founder's network. Days 21-45: community-based recruiting + first partner deals closing.

## Owner
Builder Relations Lead, with founder doing the first 3-5 recruiting conversations personally.

## KPIs
- Partners signed up
- Partners with at least 1 closed/in-progress deal (the real activation metric)
- Time from signup to first deal attempt

## Budget
Founder/BR-lead time only; commission is paid from closed-deal revenue, not upfront cash.

## Kill criteria
If signed-up partners aren't attempting a first deal within 2 weeks of joining, the starter kit is broken — fix that before recruiting more partners.

# Campaign: AI Visibility Launch Push

Owner skill: [ai-visibility](../skills/ai-visibility/SKILL.md)

Priority tier: 1 (primary revenue)

## Objective
Book 10 AI Visibility Audits and convert 3 to paid Optimization within 30 days.

## Audience
Local service business owners: dental, medical, cosmetic clinics, wellness, beauty, physiotherapy, chiropractors. Start with 1-2 verticals where a founder has existing warm relationships or easy access (fastest path to first closed deals), expand once the pitch is proven.

## Bottleneck being addressed
No proof asset exists yet. Prospects won't believe "AI visibility" matters until they see their own business missing from an actual AI answer.

## Core message/hook
"Ask ChatGPT for the best [specialty] near you. If your practice doesn't come up, neither do your next patients."

## Channels (ranked)
1. Direct outbound (founder-led) to warm/near-warm local business contacts — highest conversion at this stage.
2. LinkedIn founder posts showing real (anonymized or permissioned) audit screenshots.
3. Local business referral asks from any closed client.

## Assets needed
- Cold outreach email (see [../templates/email.md](../templates/email.md))
- One-pager / landing page for the Audit offer (see [../templates/landing-page.md](../templates/landing-page.md))
- 3-5 LinkedIn posts featuring real audit findings (see [../templates/linkedin.md](../templates/linkedin.md))
- First case study once a client converts (see [../templates/case-study.md](../templates/case-study.md))

## Timeline
Days 1-3: build audit process + one-pager. Days 4-14: outbound to first 20-30 targets. Days 15-30: deliver audits, convert to Optimization, publish first case study.

## Owner
Founder (sales + audit delivery) — do not delegate the first 10 closes to anyone else; the founder needs the raw signal from these conversations.

## KPIs
- Audits booked (target: 10)
- Audit → Optimization conversion (target: 30%+)
- Time from outreach to booked audit (track, aim to shrink)

## Budget
Founder time only. No paid spend until the pitch and delivery process are proven.

## Kill criteria
If fewer than 3 audits book after 30 targeted outreach attempts, stop and revisit positioning/ICP before sending more volume.

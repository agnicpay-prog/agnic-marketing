# Email Templates

See the [copywriter](../skills/copywriter/SKILL.md) skill for line-level copy rules.

## Cold outreach — AI Visibility (tier 1)

```
Subject: found [Business Name] missing from ChatGPT

Hi [Name],

I asked ChatGPT for the best [specialty] in [city] this morning — [Business Name] didn't come up.

That's probably costing you patients who never call because they never see you.

We do a quick (free/low-cost) audit that shows exactly what AI search sees when someone looks for a practice like yours, and what's missing.

Want me to send you yours? Takes us about [X days], no obligation.

[First name]
```

## Audit delivered → Optimization pitch (tier 1)

```
Subject: your AI visibility audit is ready

Hi [Name],

Here's what we found: [1-2 concrete findings, e.g. "you're not listed on 3 of the 5 directories AI models cross-check" / "you have 4 reviews from 2019 — recent review volume is one of the biggest factors"].

The good news: these are fixable, and most practices we've optimized start showing up within [X weeks].

Here's what Optimization includes: [3 bullets, concrete].

Want to get started? [CTA — book a call / reply yes]

[First name]
```

```

You bring the client, we do the work, you keep [X%].

I put together a starter kit (outreach template + sample audit) so you can close your first deal this week if you want to try it.

Want me to send it over?

[First name]
```

```
Subject: your first deal — anything blocking it?

Hi [Name],

Checking in — have you had a chance to reach out to a client about the Audit yet?

If it's useful, happy to jump on a call and help you close the first one together.

[First name]
```

## Post-workshop follow-up (tier 1, non-converters)

```
Subject: your AI visibility audit (from [workshop name])

Hi [Name],

Thanks for coming to [workshop]. Here's the offer we talked about: [1-line recap].

If you want us to run your audit, just reply "yes" and we'll get started this week.

[First name]
```

## Rules for all emails
- One idea, one CTA, no more than 6 short paragraphs.
- Subject lines: specific and curious, never "Unlock" / "Discover" / "Transform."
- Always personalize the first line with something true and specific about the recipient.

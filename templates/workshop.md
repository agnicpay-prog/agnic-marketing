# Workshop Agenda Template

See the [events](../skills/events/SKILL.md) skill for strategy and when to run one.

## "Is Your Practice Invisible to AI?" — Tier 1 Workshop

```
Duration: 45-60 minutes
Audience: 15-20 local business owners (dental/medical/cosmetic/wellness/beauty/physio/chiro)

0:00-0:05  Welcome + the question: "Have you ever asked ChatGPT to recommend a business like yours?"
0:05-0:20  Live demo: ask ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini for "best [specialty] near [city]" live, using 2-3 volunteer businesses from the room. Show what comes up (or doesn't).
0:20-0:35  Why this happens: structured data, reviews, directory consistency, content — explained in plain language, no jargon.
0:35-0:45  What fixing it looks like: before/after case study, timeline, what's involved.
0:45-0:55  Q&A
0:55-0:60  CTA: "We'll audit your practice for free/[price] this week if you sign up now" — collect names on the spot.
```

## "Sell AI Visibility as a Partner" — Tier 2 Workshop/Webinar

```
Duration: 45 minutes
Audience: AI builders, consultants, automation agencies

0:00-0:05  Welcome + the offer: "Add a sellable service to your existing client relationships, no build required."
0:05-0:20  Live demo: run an audit on a sample business, show what the deliverable looks like.
0:20-0:30  The economics: commission structure, what delivery Agnic handles vs. what the partner handles.
0:30-0:40  The first-deal starter kit walkthrough: outreach template, objection handling.
0:40-0:45  Q&A + sign-up CTA.
```

## Rules
- Always record it — becomes founder-brand content afterward (route clips to the [community](../skills/community/SKILL.md) skill).
- Always end with one explicit CTA and a way to say yes on the spot (sign-up sheet, QR code, form).
- Follow up with non-converters within 48 hours (see [../templates/email.md](../templates/email.md)).

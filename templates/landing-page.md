# Landing Page Template

See the [copywriter](../skills/copywriter/SKILL.md) skill for line-level copy rules.

## Structure

```
1. HOOK
[One sentence that makes the visitor's problem visible/uncomfortable]
e.g. "Ask ChatGPT for the best [specialty] near you. If you don't show up, neither does your next patient."

2. PROOF IT'S REAL
[A real example, screenshot, or number showing the problem exists]
e.g. "We checked 50 local clinics. 38 of them were invisible to ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini."

3. WHAT WE DO (one sentence, plain mechanism)
e.g. "We audit what AI search sees when someone looks for a business like yours — then fix what's missing."

4. HOW IT WORKS (3 steps max)
Step 1: [Audit — what happens, how fast]
Step 2: [Findings — what they get]
Step 3: [Optimization — what changes, what to expect]

5. PROOF
[Case study snippet, screenshot of before/after, or testimonial with a number attached]

6. OFFER
[Exact price or next step — no "contact us for pricing" if avoidable]

7. CTA
[One button, one action — "Get my free audit" not "Learn more"]

8. OBJECTION HANDLING (2-3 real objections)
"Isn't this just SEO?" → [concrete answer]
"How is this different from [competitor/status quo]?" → [concrete answer]
"How fast will I see results?" → [concrete answer]
```

## Variants to maintain

- **AI Visibility Audit landing page** (tier 1, primary — this is the page that should get the most iteration).
- **Builder Partner Program landing page** (tier 2 — sells the commission/starter-kit offer, not the end-customer product).
- **Agentic Commerce / thought-leadership page** (tier 3 — lighter touch, mostly a content hub + waitlist, not a hard sales page).

## Checklist
- [ ] Could this hook apply to any other AI startup? If yes, rewrite with a real specific.
- [ ] Is there exactly one CTA repeated (not competing CTAs)?
- [ ] Is the price/next step explicit, not hidden behind "contact us"?
- [ ] Does the proof section have a real number?
